Riverside Park Reopens After Major Renovation

By Marcus Seager

October 7, 2025

After two years of construction and community consultation, Riverside Park welcomed visitors back this week with new walkways, native plant gardens, and a restored wetland area.

Residents gathered at the park's main entrance on Monday to celebrate the official reopening. The renovation — funded by the city and private donors — includes widened paths for accessibility, improved lighting, and several areas designed to support pollinators and bird life.
