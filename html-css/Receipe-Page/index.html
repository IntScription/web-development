Chicken Fried Rice

You need to have chicken mainly, then pre-cooked rice.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Rice
  • Veggies
  • Cooking oil

Instructions

  1. Fry 2-3 chicken breasts then chop them into small fragments.
  2. Pre-heat the frying pan and add some cooking oil.
  3. Use one day old pre-cooked rice and add them to the frying pan along with the cooked and chopped chicken.
  4. Add necessary veggies and wait till its cooked, you can add soya sauce if needed or else add 2-3 pinch salts.
  5. Finally, your fried rice is ready.
